# Coffee-Shop-Sales-Analysis
Solving one of the biggest marketing problems for coffee shops, customer preferences based on seasonality and trends. This project uses data to explore insights and provide marketing recommendations for and NYC coffee shop.

 ### Project Overview

- Businesses often struggle with promoting the right products at the right time. Customer preferences change across seasons, yet many cafés rely on intuition rather than data when deciding advertising and product focus.

- This project applies data analytics and machine learning to identify which coffee shop products generate the most revenue by season and to recommend actionable marketing strategies based on predictive insights.

- The goal was to transform transactional sales data into decision-making tools that support revenue growth and smarter advertising.
